Glossaries of Acronyms

The information displayed on this site is obtained directly from the central database used by administrators within the various University of Hawaiʻi System campuses. The information may include abbreviations and acronyms whose meaning is not immediately apparent to non-administrators.

A few campuses have provided glossaries of some of their acronyms and abbreviations. These lists may not be exhaustive, and even where a term is defined here its relevance may be unclear. The solution in all cases is to talk to an academic advisor to resolve questions.
